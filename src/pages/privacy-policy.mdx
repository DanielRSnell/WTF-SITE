# Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.<br />
WooThatsFast collects only the information necessary to provide our WooCommerce hosting and optimization services. This includes contact information, website performance data, and server metrics needed to optimize your store.<br />
We do not share your data with third parties, and all data is handled securely. Your WooCommerce store data and performance metrics are kept confidential and used solely for optimization purposes.<br />
For any questions or concerns about your data, feel free to [contact us](mailto:hello@woothatsfast.com).

## GDPR Compliance

If you're located in the EU, you have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To make a request, simply [contact us](mailto:hello@woothatsfast.com). We process data in accordance with the GDPR and only retain it as long as necessary for providing WooCommerce optimization services.

## Cookies

Our site uses essential cookies to enable basic functionality like client portal access and performance dashboard preferences. We may also use cookies to understand how visitors use the site to improve our WooCommerce optimization services.

## WooCommerce Performance Data

As part of our optimization services, we collect and analyze:
- Website performance metrics and Core Web Vitals scores
- Server resource usage and database performance data
- WooCommerce-specific optimization opportunities
- This data is used exclusively to improve your store's performance and is never shared with third parties

## Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your data:
- SSL encryption for all data transmission
- Secure server environments with regular security updates
- Limited access to performance data on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security audits and monitoring
